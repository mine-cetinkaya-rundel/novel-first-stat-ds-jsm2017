# Danny Kaplan - Macalester College

Danny Kaplan is DeWitt Wallace Professor of Mathematics, Statistics, and Computer Science at Macalester College. He's authored several textbooks, including *Statistical Modeling: A Fresh Approach* and *Data Computing*. He's one of the developers of the mosaic package for R. Along with Ben Baumer and Nick Horton, he co-authored the recently released "Modern Data Science with R."
